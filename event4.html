CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - ECHOES OF LINGO (Open-mic)

ABSTRACT

Language is not solely limited to English; there are also many languages by which people can connect with each other. Every language whether it is Hindi, Urdu, French or any regional language (Maithili, Bhojpuri, Magahi etc.) is a step towards communication across cultural boundaries. So, advocate your thoughts because language is not a barrier.

Amity Institute of English Studies and Research, is organizing an open mic in which participants are encouraged to speak in any language. It could be English, French, Hindi, Bengali, or any regional language. Open mic event is meant as a delightful way to express yourself, either through stand-up comedy, poetry, or many more.

1. Concept of the event

It is a Multilingual Open Mic Contest

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

3. Rules and Regulations (for offline)

  • The time limit of Lingo will be 3 to 5 minutes.
  • The distribution of points will be based on body language, facial expressions, intonation and choice of words.
  • Participants can use any language of their choice maintaining dignity.
  • Use of unparliamentary words and distasteful language will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the jury will stand final, no further clarifications will be entertained.
  • Participants are welcome to bring a written piece to read from, but extra applause goes to those who can perform without the aid of external assistance.
  • Two reminders will be given regarding time; one after 3 minutes and one before 30 seconds.

4. Rules and Regulations (for online)

  • There are 2 categories: senior and junior.
  • The competition is open to all currently enrolled students from Amity University and any other recognized school or college.
  • Participation is individual.
  • The time limit will be 3 to 5 minutes.
  • The participants will have to send their recorded videos to the respective email ID of the event.
  • Distribution of points will be based on body language, facial expressions, intonation and choice of words.
  • Use of unparliamentary words and distasteful language will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the jury will stand final, no further clarifications will be entertained.
  • Videos must be submitted in one continuous take, without any cuts or edits during the performance.
  • Technical difficulties such as poor audio or video quality may lead to elimination.
  • Participants must adhere to the specified submission deadline for their entries. The deadline for video submission is 21st April 2024 in the following mail id echoesoflingo@gmail.com
  • Late submission will not be accepted. Participants must submit their respective videos before the deadline.

5. Registration

  • Registration fee is only Rs.100 (excluding GST).
  • Once the registration is done, no refund will be initiated.
  • Registration is Compulsory for all the participants through the link or QR Code.
  • Not abiding by any instructions will lead to disqualification.
  • Certificate of Participation for all.
  • Certificate of Merit and prizes for the winners.
