CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - PIRATES OF LITSVILLA (Treasure hunt) [ Will Conducted Offline Only ]

ABSTRACT

The Amity Institute of English Studies and Research invites you to an exceptional treasure hunt that lures around the corners of fun amalgamated in literature. Unravel the enchanting power that literature holds; you'll get to experience the amazing things that literature can do in this upgraded version Carnivalesque 3.0 #Pirates of LitsVilla. "I won't call it a treasure hunt, but it's like an old Western movie where you're onto one thing and it turns into another" ~Aaron Brookner. As you set out on your quest, remember, there are clues to guide you, each one pointing you in the direction of the next. And as you follow these clues, a picture will begin to form in your mind, revealing the path to the ultimate prize. But beware, for this is no ordinary treasure hunt, it will require all your skills and craft to decipher the hidden messages and uncover the mystery. In the end, it's not about the treasure itself, but the thrill of the chase. So, gear up and get ready to test your wits, because the hunt is on and time is running out. Will you be the one to find the treasure, or will you be the one hunted!

1. Concept of the event

There will be two rounds. The first round will be the screening round followed by the final round. In the screening round pirates have to find the tickets and that will lead them to the final round.

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

3. Rules and Regulations

  • A team should only have minimum two and maximum five members.
  • Each team will be given a list of items, challenges, or riddles that must be found or completed. The goal is to find all the items or complete all challenges on the list.
  • Clues have to be collected by the pirates.
  • The language used in treasure hunt should be English.
  • The use of mobile phone during the competition is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the jury will stand final; no further clarification will be entertained.

4. Registration

  • Registration fee is only Rs.100 (excluding GST).
  • Once the registration is done, no refund will be initiated.
  • Registration for all the participants is compulsory through link or QR code.
  • Not abiding by any instructions will lead to disqualification.
  • Registration will be done individually not in group i.e. each individual has to register separately.
  • Certificate of Participation for all.
  • Certificate of Merit and prizes for the winners.
