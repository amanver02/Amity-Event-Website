CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - CON-CLOSET (Cosplay)

ABSTRACT

Step into the enchanting realm of Amity University Patna as we unveil the grand stage for CON-CLOSET, our captivating Cosplay competition. Join us for an unparalleled spectacle where creativity knows no bounds. Prepare to immerse yourself in a world of boundless creativity, where participants will don enchanting attire inspired by beloved characters from literature, pop culture and beyond. Let your imagination soar as we embark on this extraordinary journey of self-expression and artistry. "In a world where imagination knows no bounds, cosplay becomes the ultimate portal to step into the realms of our beloved character".

1. Concept of the event

Participants are expected to dress up in special costumes resembling or inspired by favorite characters or from literature and pop culture.

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

3. Rules and Regulations (for offline)

  • No real weapons allowed (inflammables, Sharp objects etc.).
  • Decency in clothing should be maintained else it will lead to disqualification.
  • Each participant will get only 2-3 minutes for the performance.
  • Participants are advised to share any audio or video or both if required for their performance a day prior to the event.
  • Refreshments will be provided by the university.
  • Cafeteria will remain open for all.
  • The use of mobile phones during the competition is strictly prohibited.
  • Online registration is compulsory for each participant.
  • The decision of the jury will stand final, no further clarifications will be entertained.

4. Rules and Regulations (for online)

  • This competition is open to all the students.
  • Duration of the performance is maximum 2-3 minutes.
  • Allotment of Marks will be done on the basis of: Successful portrayal of the Performance in acting (dialogue delivery, expressions and body language).
  • Videos must be submitted in one continuous take, without any cuts or edits during the performance.
  • The videos are to be submitted by April 21, 2024 in the following mail id concloset.aiesr@gmail.com.
  • Technical difficulties such as poor audio or video quality may lead to elimination.
  • Use of non-parliamentary words, slangs or distasteful language will lead to disqualification.
  • Scripts must not touch on sensitive issues or target any particular race, religion, or politics.

  • It is mandatory for teams to be from the same school/college.
  • Participants must adhere to the specified submission deadline for their entries.

5. Registration

  • Registration fee is only Rs.100 (excluding GST).
  • Once the registration is done, no refund will be initiated.
  • Registration is compulsory for all the participants through link or QR code.
  • Not abiding by any instructions will lead to disqualification.
  • Registration will be done individually not in group i.e. each individual has to register separately.
  • Certificate of Participation for all.
  • Certificate of Merit and prizes for the winners.
