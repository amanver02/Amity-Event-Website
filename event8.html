CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - Millennial Theatre (Play)

ABSTRACT

Do you ever find yourself itching to rewrite the script of a movie you just watched or reshape the narrative of a book you just finished? That longing to step into the shoes of your favourite character can now become a reality. Welcome to Millennial Theatre, where we invite you to take the stage and breathe new life into stories, igniting deeper emotions and catharsis in a captivated audience. In this electrifying showdown, will you emerge as the reigning champion, captivating the audience and claiming the spot in the limelight? It’s time to step up, stand out, and let your talent speak volumes in this battle of wits and entertainment.

1. Concept of the event

A competition involving skits that promote crushing of the stereotypes and shall seek to reform the unspoken rules of society.

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

3. Rules and Regulations (for offline)

  • This competition is open to all the students.
  • Duration of stage performance is limited to 10-12 minutes.
  • The team must constitute of neither less than three nor more than seven members.
  • Allotment of Marks will be done on the basis of: Successful portrayal of the theme, Performance in acting (dialogue delivery, expressions and body language), coordination between members, and the overall impact of the script and rendition of script.
  • Use of non-parliamentary words, slangs or distasteful language will lead to disqualification.
  • Scripts must not touch on sensitive issues or target any particular race, religion, or politics.
  • Costumes and props are to be of decent nature.
  • Use of sharp objects, fire, inflammable and dangerous chemical substances are strictly prohibited.
  • Personal belongings are to be looked after by individuals or teams.
