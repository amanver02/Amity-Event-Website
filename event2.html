CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - DRAMICS (Comic Strip)

ABSTRACT

Welcome to Dramics, where creativity knows no bounds! Step into the arena of artistic expression and unleash your imagination in an electrifying drawing competition open to all.Let your unique style shine as you captivate with your innovative creations.

1. Concept of the event

In this competition, participants are supposed to draw a comic strip narrating a story or concept based on the theme chosen at the time of the registration.

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

4. Rules and Regulations (for offline)

  • Sheet: The organisers will provide a glaze sheet to participants to draw on.
  • Medium: Any dry medium is allowed (e.g., graphite pencils, charcoal, coloured pencils, pastels). Wet mediums (watercolours, acrylics, etc.) are not permitted. Digital artwork is not accepted for this competition.
  • Originality: All artwork must be original and created by the participant. No plagiarism or copying will be allowed.
  • The decision of the jury will stand final, no further clarifications will be entertained.
  • The use of mobile phones during the competition is strictly prohibited.

5. Rules and Regulations (for online)

  • There are 2 categories: senior and junior.
  • The competition is open to all currently enrolled students from Amity University and any other recognized school or college.
  • Participation is individual.
  • Participants will choose a topic from the list of topics provided.
  • Along with the drawing, participants must provide a Comic strips, which means that a sentence or a dialogue regarding the comic drawn can be added in the drawings.
  • To maintain authenticity and fairness in the competition, participants are required to submit their identity cards.
  • Participants are required to create individual PDF for each submission.
  • PDF files should be named appropriately as participants name mentioned in their specific identity cards.
  • Late submission will not be accepted. Participants must submit their respective work before the deadline.
  • Participants must adhere to the specified submission deadline for their entries. The deadline for pdf submission is 21st April 2024.
