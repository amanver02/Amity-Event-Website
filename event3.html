CARNIVALESQUE 3.0 - QUIZZARD (Quiz) [ Will Conducted Offline only ]

ABSTRACT

Does literature captivate you or does the vibrant cinematic world entice you? Rewinding your thoughts of knowledge is what we call Quizzard. This is no ordinary quiz. Amity Institute of English Studies and Research offers you to celebrate and test your spectrum of knowledge from the world of movies, novels, series, music, and many more. A thrilling adventure and a glimpse of a euphoric literary ride. Get enchanted by famous literary works from around the world. This is just a glimpse of the great literary roller coaster ride you are about to experience!

1. Concept of the event

In round one the question will be asked from, English literature like poetry, novels, famous quotes, popular dramas, etc. Round two will be conducted through a PowerPoint presentation, the questions will be asked from different popular series, and their characters, guess the famous dialogue, etc. and many more exciting questions.

2. Eligibility

For the junior group, it is 13 to 17 years of age. For the senior group, it is 17+ years of age. A separate category for Amity students.

3. Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must go through 2 rounds
  • Candidates will be allotted 1 hour to solve 35 questions in the first round
  • For each correct answer, 1 point will be rewarded and no negative marking in round 1
  • In the second round, 5 points will be rewarded, and for every wrong answer, 1 point will be deducted
  • Ten participants with the highest score will be eligible for the second round
  • In the second round, the rules will vary according to the rounds and will be informed on the spot by the quiz master
  • 6 candidates will be selected for the final round: 2 groups, each containing 3 members
  • The language used should be English
  • The use of mobile phones during the competition is strictly prohibited
  • The jury's decision will stand final, no further clarifications will be entertained
  • Not abiding by any of the instructions will lead to disqualification
